About

A life shaped by music,
inspired by nature

Born into a family of musicians, Russian-Canadian pianist Nikolai Nesterov brings a deep passion for music and the arts to every performance, lesson, recording, and image he captures.

Currently pursuing a Master of Music in Piano Performance at the University of Montréal under Henry Kramer, he previously completed his Bachelor of Music at the University of Calgary and trained at the Mount Royal Conservatory’s Academy of Music for the Gifted Youth and its Advanced Performance Program. Nikolai has performed with the Calgary Philharmonic Orchestra, appeared in concert series such as ProArts and Music @ Noon, and is an active chamber musician with the Peaks + Prairies Ensemble. Alongside performing, he is a dedicated piano teacher and also explores photography, computer programming, and the outdoors.
