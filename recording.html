Professional Services

Recording Services

High-quality audio and video recordings for musicians, competition submissions, auditions, and professional applications.

About the Service

Crafted for the
serious musician

As both a performing musician and experienced photographer, I understand exactly what is needed to present yourself at your very best on recordings. Whether it's a competition submission, a conservatory application, or a professional portfolio piece — every detail matters.

I offer recordings in a professional setting using high-quality microphones and cameras, delivering results that are clean, balanced, and true to your artistry. Sessions can be tailored entirely to your needs — from a single piece to a full recital programme.

Sessions are available in Calgary by appointment. Reach out by email to discuss availability, repertoire, and to receive a personalised quote.

Professional-grade
tools and setup

Professional Microphones
Studio-grade condenser microphones capturing every nuance of your sound with precision and warmth.

Service Options

Two ways to
work together

Every musician's needs are different. Choose the option that works best for your schedule, workflow, and goals.

Option 01
Raw Files Delivery

Ideal for musicians who prefer to handle post-production themselves, or who already have an editor or audio engineer they work with. You receive all original files from the session — unedited, full quality.

What you receive
Original, unedited audio file(s) — full quality
Original, unedited video file(s) — full resolution
All recorded takes from your session
Delivered via secure file transfer

What you receive
Professional audio recording & editing
